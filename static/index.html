How it works

End-to-end AI voice sales workflow.

  1. Read leads from Excel. Only pending leads are called; completed and opted-out are skipped.
  2. AI voice call. Click Call. The agent speaks aloud (browser voice) and listens with your mic — or you can type the prospect's replies for a mic-free demo.
  3. Natural sales conversation. The agent introduces the company, qualifies the prospect, answers from the knowledge base, and handles objections.
  4. Meeting booking. If interested, it collects a day & time and marks the meeting booked.
  5. Excel CRM update. On End call, call status, qualification, summary, requirements, objections, follow-up and meeting time are written back to the sheet.

Set OPENAI_API_KEY to use a live LLM; without it, a built-in rule-based sales brain runs so the app works with zero configuration.
