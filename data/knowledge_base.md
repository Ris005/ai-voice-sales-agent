# Nimbus CRM — Company Knowledge Base

## What we do
Nimbus CRM is an AI-first sales CRM for small and mid-sized teams. It automates
lead follow-up, logs every call and email, and tells reps who to contact next.

## Core features
- **Unified inbox** — calls, WhatsApp, email, and web chat in one timeline.
- **AI call summaries** — every call is transcribed and summarized automatically.
- **Smart follow-ups** — the system schedules and drafts the next touch for you.
- **Pipeline & forecasting** — drag-and-drop deals, live revenue forecast.
- **Excel / Google Sheets sync** — two-way sync, no data lock-in.
- **Open API + Zapier** — connect existing tools in minutes.

## Pricing (INR, per user / month, billed annually)
- **Starter** — ₹499. Up to 3 users, 1,000 contacts, core CRM.
- **Growth** — ₹999. Unlimited contacts, AI summaries, pipeline forecasting.
- **Scale** — ₹1,999. Multi-team, advanced automation, priority support, SSO.
- 14-day free trial on every plan. No credit card required.

## Common questions
- **Onboarding time?** Most teams are live in under a day; we import from Excel/Sheets.
- **Contract lock-in?** No. Monthly or annual; cancel anytime, export your data anytime.
- **Data security?** Encrypted at rest and in transit; India + EU data residency options.
- **Support?** Email + chat on all plans; phone/priority on Scale.
- **Does it replace our phone system?** No — it layers on top and logs the calls.

## Ideal customer
Sales teams of 2–50 people currently juggling spreadsheets, WhatsApp, and a legacy
CRM they find clunky. Biggest wins: less manual data entry and no dropped follow-ups.
